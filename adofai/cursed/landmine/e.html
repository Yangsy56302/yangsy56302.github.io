ADOFAI Landmine Curse Effects
by 来者小俊4412＿多米诺 (Xiaojun4412)

Notes that IDs and parameters are NOT parts of the official settings.

ADOFAI Cursed Levels by Xiaojun4412

1-X* A Dance of Fire and Ice-但直角变成了发卡弯-
Unofficial translation: -but right angles turns into turnarounds-
The initial tile position should not be changed in order to keep the track shape.
(The restriction above doesn't includes rotation. Moving tile position when planets are nearby is also allowed.)
2-X* Offbeats-但有角度的部分都少一拍-
Unofficial translation: -but the angled parts are 1 beat shorter-
有角度的部分 stands for 180° tiles somehow --
the actual effect is to remove an 180° tile pre straight line (the last one by default).
3-X* The Wind-Up-但弧度为nπ/4(内圈,n∈{1,2,3})时都有一个长条-
Unofficial translation: -but when the radian is nπ/4 (n∈{1,2,3}) there's a hold-
4-X* Love Letters-但角度为90°时为双押-
Unofficial translation: -but when the degree is 90° it's a doubletap-
5-X* The Midnight Train-但只能走外圈-
Unofficial translation: -but only move on the side that's >180°-
Twirls will be added on tiles thats its angle is <180°, therefore as result, all the tiles should now be >180°.
6-X* Pulse-但是三球和原轨道-
Unofficial translation: -but triple planets & original track-
For another level that its curse effect was the same with 6-X*, but made by Yangsy56302, see "XN-X" .
Change the planet counts to 3, without changing the shape of the chart, and the duration for 180° tiles.
7-X* Spin 2 Win-但轨道为直角（外圈）时都有一个长条-
Unofficial translation: -but when tile is 270° there's a hold-
8-X* Jungle City-但每经过n边形时在其里循环n次-
Unofficial translation: -but n-polygon repeats themselves for n times-
9-X* Classic Pursuit-但12-X螃蟹在这里-
Unofficial translation: -but 12-X crab is here-
For the newer version of 9-X landmine curse, see 9-XN* .
This curse is an alias of 12-X? .
9-XN* Classic Pursuit-但角度为特定角度时暂停一拍-
Unofficial translation: -but when the angle matches specific angles there's a 1 beat pause-
For the older version of 9-X landmine curse, see 9-X* .
10-X* Butterfly Planet-但判定限制在变-
Unofficial translation: -but the judgement limit is changing-
Pure Perfect: 70% Judgement
All Perfect: 80% Judgement
Full Combo: 90% Judgement
Standard: 100% Judgement
11-X* Heracles-但每个封闭图形都少一个边-
Unofficial translation: -but every enclosed shapes has lost 1 side-
The result is undefined when applying on triangles since Xiaojun4412 never[Citation needed] used this curse on sections with triangles.
12-X* Artificial Chariot-但有太多个正方形-
Unofficial translation: -but there's way too many squares-
Randomly add (both inside and outside) squares to the track, usually at places with atleast 1 right angle.
RJ-X* Fear Grows-但双押与单键互换-
Unofficial translation: -but doubletaps and singletaps are swapped-
XF-X* Third Wave Flip-Flop-但只能走斜步-
Unofficial translation: -but skewed steps only-
XH-X* Final Hope-但每经过n边形时加0.01n倍速-
Unofficial translation: -but each n-polygon passed would speeds you up for n%-
PA-X* Distance-但每个封闭图形的方块都变成了发卡弯-
Unofficial translation: -but tiles belongs to enclosed shapes turns into turnarounds-
This level has its own curse effect which uses the same ID.
For another level without that, see No Distance.
The initial tile position should not be changed in order to keep the track shape.
(The restriction above doesn't includes rotation. Moving tile position when planets are nearby is also allowed.)
XC-X* Credits-但兔子与蜗牛互换-
Unofficial translation: -but the rabbits and the snails are swapped-
This level has its own curse effect which uses the same ID.
For another level without that, see Explodits.
XN-X* Trans-Neptunian Object-但每个外圈轨道的中间位置都有一个中旋-
Unofficial translation: -but there's a midspin at the middle of each tile that's >180°-
Although the existence of this curse effect was confirmed, the corresponding level never show its existence on Steam Workshop nor Bilibili video. Not sure why.
XM-X* Miko Skip-但发卡弯不见了-
Unofficial translation: -but the turnarounds are gone-
Tiles that was adjacent to an turnaround would still looks the same as before (like a fork in the road), but acts like 1 single tile.
XR-X* Rose Garden-但每个旋转都会改变星球的数量-
Unofficial translation: -but each twirl would change the planet count-
This level has its own curse effect which uses the same ID.
For another level without that, see "Landmine Garden".
XS-X* Party of spirits-但每个封闭图形都出问题了-
Unofficial translation: -but there's something wrong with each enclosed shape-
This level has its own curse effect which uses the same ID.
For another level without that, see Party of landmine.

ADOFAI cursed DLC levels by Xiaojun4412

T1-EX* NEW LIFE-但每经过一个长条会让你更累-
Unofficial translation: -but each hold makes you more tired-
This is similar to XR-X? , which did slows down the speed at first, but suddenly switched into speeding up in halfway.
T2-X* sing sing red indigo-但中旋变成了发卡弯-
Unofficial translation: -but midspins turns into turnarounds-
T4-EX* Third Sun-但谱师在制作关卡时破坏了DLC-
Unofficial translation: -but the charter broke the DLC when making level-
  • Hold string are stretched and broken at middle, hold start turns into a 1 beat pause;
  • Special midspins works normally;
  • Freeroam behavior is undefined;
  • Multiplanet do spawn an extra planet, but it stays still instead of moves into orbit.
