"T5-X" also accept composited curses and reverted curses.
The but statement of composited curses should be formed by simply concatenating 2 but statements of both curses in affecting order.

Current curse: "T5-X" ( "3-X" + "4-X", "straight line", "midspin" )

Current curse: "T5-X" ( - "8-X", "same-angled", "straight" )
