ADOFAI String Cursed Levels by Yangsy56302

General

The curses should be hinted by using text decorations on the bottom of the interface:

Current curse by Yangsy56302: 10-X + 11-X
Next curse by Yangsy56302: 12-X

Curse effects is usually regarded as a chart-to-chart mapping at here.

Curse effects can stack: the order is from left to right. Curse effects can also stack on themselves.

Anticurse (like) can be used to reverse the effect of a curse, as long as that part is possible to have an reversed version which is possible to form the current part if you use the original curse to it.
(when i said reverse, i didn't mean simply negate the values, although it may be equivalent; i mean negate the whole process)

In the following content, midspins will be considered as 0° angle.

Main Game

1-X

Stricted Judgement:
Green ones are allowed;
Yellow ones are allowed by the curse but you will be killed by judgement itself;
Red ones are NOT allowed and you will be killed instantly by the curse.

2-X

Rhythm Change:
3/4, 7/8, 4/5, etc.

3-X

Straights Extention:
You starts with N = 0.
Every time you reached the end of a chain of 180° tiles, the tile right after the chain needs to hit N extra times;
After that, N increased by 1.

4-X

Short:
Remove segments at any scale.

5-X

April Fools:
The planets are in the wrong orientation yet the chart plays the exact same as it did before.

6-X

Pause:
Doubling the BPM and increasing paused beat counts by 1.

7-X

Inner Ring = Speed Down, Outer Ring = Speed Up.

8-X

Curved Charts:
Every chain of NOT 180° tiles will be extended by 1.
(0° and 360° may also counts, it depends. this effect can also adding or removing twirls on a 180° tile for better readability)

9-X

Every tile that its angle is not an integer multiple of 90° will have a twirl, even if it already has one.

10-X

Twirls will be added on a tile when its angle is bigger than 180°.
(this effect can also adding or removing twirls on a 180° tile for better readability)

11-X

Red Twirl = Speed Up, Blue Twirl = Speed Down.

12-X

Mirror Symmetry:
The angle of each tile is now its supplementary angle.
(integer multiples of 180° will bypass this curse; angles bigger than 180°will be moduled by 180° when processing. this effect can also adding or removing twirls for better readability)
