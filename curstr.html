General

In the following content, midspins will be considered as 0° angle.

Basics

Each ADOFAI level may correspond to an curse effect (theoretically).
The IDs of the two are the same.

If a part of the chart cannot match the pre-conditions of the curse effect it's using, nothing (that does matters)> will happen.

Using a curse effect may produces many possibilities of results.
Just choose a specific one to use.

Formats

The curses should be hinted by using text decorations on the bottom of the interface:

Current curse by Yangsy56302: 12-X
Next curse by Yangsy56302: XT-X

(and yes, that's why the name of this cursed levels series is string!)

There will be a countdown before updating the current curse effects:
The countdown starts with 3, decreases by 1 in equal interval time. The curse effects will be updated at the exact same time when the number should be counted down to -1.

Current curse by Yangsy56302: ML-X
Next curse by Yangsy56302: 5-X (3)
Current curse by Yangsy56302: ML-X
Next curse by Yangsy56302: 5-X (2)
Current curse by Yangsy56302: ML-X
Next curse by Yangsy56302: 5-X (1)
Current curse by Yangsy56302: ML-X
Next curse by Yangsy56302: 5-X (0)
Current curse by Yangsy56302: 5-X
Next curse by Yangsy56302: 2-X

Keywords

... stands for "please wait" for nexts, and infrequently, "need more info" for currents .

Current curse by Yangsy56302: ...
Next curse by Yangsy56302: ...

None literally stands for "no effects".
StopIteration stands for "that was the last curse state change, you can take a break now".

Current curse by Yangsy56302: None
Next curse by Yangsy56302: StopIteration

Mechanisms

Basics

Curse effects is usually regarded as a chart-to-chart mapping at here.

Curse effects can stack, represents with a plus sign between:

Current curse by Yangsy56302: XR-X + 12-X
Next curse by Yangsy56302: 12-X + XR-X

Advanced

The affect order of curse effects in hint text decorations is from left to right.
For example:

XR-X + 12-X will first add triangles,
then try to change the angle of every tile,
including those tiles that is a part of these new triangle,
so now these triangles are probably not triangles anymore;

but 12-X + XR-X will first try to change the angle of every tile,
then add triangles which will not be modified by the angle change process thats already finished.

Meta

The time order of curse effects IS NOT the affect order of curse effects.

Curse effects can also stack on themselves, and there is a abbreviation for this situation:

Current curse by Yangsy56302: XS-X + XS-X + XS-X
Next curse by Yangsy56302: XS-X*3

(color isn't required but recommend for readability)

Anticurses
Anticurses are basically the reversal of the curse process, so they can be used to reverse the effect of a curse:

the precondition is that part is possible to have an reversed version which is possible to form the current part if you use the original curse to it;
Otherwise, you can still use them, but it won't do anything when the preconditions no longer mets, and until it mets again.

Anticurse are represented by changing the minus sign to a plus sign in the ID of the corresponding curse:

Current curse by Yangsy56302: 8+X
Next curse by Yangsy56302: 9-X + 9+X

(color isn't required but recommend for readability)

Using an anticurse may produces many possibilities of results which is totally different with the original chart, or does not match expectations at all.
Just choose a specific one to use as I mentioned before.

List of Curses

Main Game (1-X ~ 12-X)

1-X
Stricted Judgement:
Green ones are allowed;
Yellow ones are allowed by the curse but you will be killed by judgement itself;
Red ones are NOT allowed and you will be killed instantly by the curse.
2-X
Rhythm Change:
3/4, 7/8, 4/5, etc.
3-X
Straights Extention:
You starts with N = 0.
Every time you reached the end of a chain of 180° tiles, the tile right after the chain needs to hit N extra times;
After that, N increased by 1.
4-X
Short:
Remove segments at any scale.
5-X
April Fools:
The planets are in the wrong orientation yet the chart plays the exact same as it did before.
6-X
Pause:
Doubling the BPM and increasing paused beat counts by 1.
7-X
Inner Ring = Speed Down, Outer Ring = Speed Up.
8-X
Curved Charts:
Every chain of NOT 180° tiles will be extended by 1.
(0° and 360° may also counts, it depends. this effect can also adding or removing twirls on a 180° tile for better readability)
9-X
Every tile that its angle is not an integer multiple of 90° will have a twirl, even if it already has one.
10-X
Twirls will be added on a tile when its angle is bigger than 180°.
(this effect can also adding or removing twirls on a 180° tile for better readability)
11-X
Red Twirl = Speed Up, Blue Twirl = Speed Down.
12-X
Mirror Symmetry:
The angle of each tile is now its supplementary angle.
(integer multiples of 180° will bypass this curse; angles bigger than 180°will be moduled by 180° when processing. this effect can also adding or removing twirls for better readability)

Xtra/Challenge (B-X, X_-X)

B-X
Every tile is doubled (except the end portal).
XF-X
If the center of two (or more) tiles was overlapped with each other, The planet will choose the shortest path (in time, not in tile count) to walk on.
(if there is a tie, either option is acceptable)
XC-X
Replacing a segment to a tutorial level, or inserting one.
XH-X
Adding DLC contents.
XR-X
If a 90° tile is not a part of an triangle, give it one.
XN-X
Multiplied the BPM by 2/3 and change the planet counts to 3, without changing the shape of the chart.
XS-X
Each event below is 1 tiles after its original position:
  • Set Speed, Twirl, Checkpoint
  • Hold, Multi Planet
  • Paused Beats, Timing Window Scale
  • Hide Judgement/Floor Icons, AutoPlay tiles (if as a part of gameplay)
  • Set Conditional Events, Set Input Event (if as a part of gameplay)
XT-X
Removed 180° tiles.
XO-1
For each 360° tiles, if there is no swirl on it, add one; otherwise remove the swirl.
XO-2
For each 180° tiles, if there is no swirl on it, add one; otherwise remove the swirl.
XO-X
The combination of XO-1 + XO-2.
XI-X
Add 2 extra paused beats to each tile with icon, except hold starts (the game refused to do that) and portal (no perceptible changes for that).

Collaboration (PA-X, RJ-X, M_-X)

PA-X
Replacing some non-midspin tiles to midspin without changing the rhythm.
RJ-X
Replacing multitaps to midspin-styled multitaps.
MN-X
Replacing each midspin to non-midspin tiles without changing the rhythm.
ML-X
Equal Division: The time length of every note in each part (section, 2 beats, beat, etc.) is averaged.
(notes that span multiple parts belongs to the first part; also it's possible to have 0 notes in some parts)
MO-X
When you hit a tile which its angle is bigger than 180°, your current speed will be doubled for once,
until you hit a tile which its angle is smaller than 180°, which will halved your current speed for once,
until you hit a tile which its angle is bigger than 180°, which will doubled your current speed for once,
until you hit a tile which its angle is smaller than 180°... okay i'll shut up.

Neo Cosmos (T_-(E)X)

T1-X

T1-EX
